# Model Card

Intended use
- Pricing and risk analytics demonstration for institutional clients

Supported instruments
- Equity and FX vanillas
- Rate options (Black-76)
- Exotics: barrier, Asian, digital (MC)
- Credit: CDS with flat hazard rate

Metrics
- Price accuracy benchmarks against analytic formulas
- Monte Carlo convergence checks

Data
- Synthetic and public data only

Risks
- Simplified assumptions
- Limited coverage of volatility surface dynamics

Version
- 0.1.0
